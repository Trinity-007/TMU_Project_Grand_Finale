Admissions

Welcome to Trinity Memorial University Admissions. Discover how to join our vibrant community of scholars.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Official high school/secondary school transcripts
  • Minimum of 5 credits including English and Mathematics
  • Recommendation letter from a teacher or counselor
  • Personal statement of purpose

How to Apply

  1. Review available programs under Academics.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Upload all required documents.
  4. Pay the application fee (if applicable).
  5. Wait for confirmation and admission decision via email.
