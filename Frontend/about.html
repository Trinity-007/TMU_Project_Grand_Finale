About Trinity Memorial University

Affordable Tuition for All

At Trinity Memorial University, we believe that quality education should not be a privilege for the few, but a right for everyone. Our mission is to provide affordable tuition so that every bright mind, especially across Africa, can access the tools they need to succeed.

While our vision was to offer completely free education, free scholarships for all, just like Power Learn Program (PLP). But then we realized that true value is often embraced when there is commitment. For this reason, TMU has designed the most affordable tuition possible, ensuring students can pursue their dreams without overwhelming financial burdens.

Why TMU?

  • Affordable tuition with flexible payment plans
  • Scholarship and financial aid opportunities
  • Global-standard programs tailored to African realities
  • Dedicated faculty committed to student success
  • Faith-driven environment that blends knowledge and character

Our Commitment

TMU is not just another university. We are a movement to redefine what higher education means in Africa. Every student admitted into TMU represents a step toward a continent where potential is not wasted simply because of financial barriers.
